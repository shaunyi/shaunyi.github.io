About

I am a principal member of technical staff AT&T Labs. Before joining AT&T, I was a teaching and research assistant at the department of computer science at the University of Georgia for 5 years. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Georgia, working with the Professor Krys Kochut. My Ph.D. dissertation is "A CPNets based Design and Analysis Framework for Service Oriented Distributed Systems" [ PDF]. My research interests are algorithms, distributed systems, computer networks, software system design and machine learning Research Statement.


  • Ph.D. in Computer Science, Department of Computer Science, The University of Georgia, 2000 - 2005

  • B.E. in Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 1992 - 1996
