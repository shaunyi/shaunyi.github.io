I am a principal member of technical staff at AT&T Labs. Before joining AT&T, I was a teaching and research assistant at the University of Georgia for 5 years. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Georgia, working with Professor Krys Kochut. My Ph.D. dissertation is "A CPNets-based Design and Analysis Framework for Service Oriented Distributed Systems". My research interests are algorithms, distributed systems, computer networks, software system design and machine learning.
